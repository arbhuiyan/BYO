Programs

Youth Programs

At BYO, we offer a variety of programs tailored to enhance the personal and social development of young people in South Shields. Our programs are designed to engage young minds and bodies in activities that build skills, character, and community connections.

Cultural Activities

To celebrate and educate about our rich heritage, we conduct cultural to museums, historical sites, and theatre shows. We also host cultural workshops where participants can learn about traditional arts, crafts, and culinary skills.

Sports Activities

Our sports programs aim to promote physical health and teamwork. We organise football, badminton, and cricket tournaments, as well as regular practice sessions under the guidance of experienced coaches.

Educational Workshops

We focus on providing workshops that enhance academic skills and offer career guidance. Topics include college preparation, study skills, and vocational training in various fields.

Youth Workshops

These are designed to address contemporary issues affecting young people, such as cyber safety, mental health awareness, and anti-bullying strategies.

Community services

BYO is deeply committed to supporting not just the youth but also the wider community through a variety of services aimed at enhancing the quality of life for all residents.

Counselling Services: We provide access to counselling and support for individuals and families dealing with stress, anxiety, and other mental health issues.

Support Groups: Regular meetings for parents, teens, and other community members facing specific challenges. These groups offer a platform for sharing experiences and receiving peer support

Community Development Initiatives: Engaging in local improvement projects that enhance communal spaces, promote environmental awareness, and foster community spirit through volunteerism.

Special Projects

BYO is involved in several special projects that have a lasting impact on our community and provide meaningful engagement opportunities for our youth.

Cultural Exchange Programs: These programs allow participants to experience and appreciate diverse cultures, fostering global awareness and tolerance.

Crime Prevention Initiative: In collaboration with local authorities, this project focuses on educating young people about the consequences of crime and providing them with positive alternatives.

Youth Leadership Training: Aimed at developing the next generation of community leaders, this project includes leadership workshops, mentorship programs, and practical leadership opportunities within the BYO and the wider community.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

1. What is the Bangladeshi Youth Organisation?

BYO is a community-centred organisation dedicated to supporting the youth of the South Shields Bangladeshi community through educational, cultural, and recreational activities.

2. How can I get involved with BYO?

We welcome everyone interested in volunteering or participating in our activities. You can sign up through our website's Volunteering page, become a member, or simply join us at one of our upcoming events.

3. What types of programs does BYO offer?

We offer a wide range of programs including educational workshops, sports tournaments, cultural activities, and community development initiatives aimed at fostering skills and enriching lives.

4. How can I make a donation to BYO?

Donations can be made directly through our website via the Donations page, where you can choose to make a one-time contribution or set up recurring support. All donations are gratefully received and go directly towards funding our projects and activities.

5. Are there any requirements to become a volunteer?

Volunteers need to complete an application form and, depending on the role, may require a DBS check. More details are available on our Volunteering page.

6. Can I participate in activities if I'm not of Bangladeshi descent?

Absolutely! BYO is inclusive and welcomes individuals from all backgrounds who are interested in participating in or supporting our activities.

7. How does BYO ensure the safety of its participants?

Safety is our top priority. We adhere to strict safeguarding policies, and all our staff and volunteers are trained to ensure a secure and supportive environment for all participants.

8. Where are BYO events held?

Our events are held at various locations throughout South Shields and sometimes at regional attractions or cultural sites. Check our Events page for information on upcoming events and locations.

9. How can I stay updated with BYO activities?

Stay connected through our website and follow us on social media. We regularly update our News/Blog section and social media channels with the latest activities and upcoming events.

10. Who can I contact for more specific questions?

For more specific inquiries, please use the Contact Us form on our website, and someone from our team will get back to you as soon as possible.

This FAQ section is designed to provide immediate answers to your questions, making it easier for you to learn about and engage with BYO. If your question isn’t covered here, please don’t hesitate to reach out.

Welcome to the Bangladeshi Youth Organisation (BYO)!


A vibrant community hub dedicated to nurturing and empowering the youth of South Shields. Since our inception, BYO has been committed to creating opportunities that foster the social, educational, and cultural development of young people, particularly those of Bangladeshi origin.

At BYO, we believe in the power of community and the potential of our youth. Our organisation serves as a dynamic platform where young individuals can engage in a variety of activities, from sports like badminton and football to educational workshops and cultural events. These programs are designed not only to entertain but also to educate and inspire our members, promoting a sense of belonging and identity.

Our mission extends beyond just recreation; we are dedicated to guiding young people away from the paths of crime and anti-social behaviour. Through targeted initiatives and supportive environments, we aim to provide alternatives that lead to positive life choices. Our efforts are rooted in the belief that every young person deserves the chance to succeed and contribute to their community in meaningful ways.

Despite the challenges brought on by the pandemic, which led to a temporary pause in our activities, BYO is bouncing back with renewed vigour. We are excited to expand our offerings and reach within the community, reintroducing old programs and introducing new ones that meet the evolving needs of our youth.

Join us at BYO, where we are more than just an organisation; we are a family committed to the growth and prosperity of our young people. Whether you are looking to participate in our activities, volunteer, or support us in other ways, your involvement is welcomed and valued. Together, we can make a lasting impact on the lives of young people in South Shields.


History

Founded in the heart of South Shields, the Bangladeshi Youth Organisation (BYO) has been a cornerstone of the community since its establishment. Originating as a safe haven for local youth of Bangladeshi descent, BYO has evolved into a vibrant center that celebrates cultural diversity and supports the holistic development of young people. We have continuously adapted to meet the changing needs of our members, expanding our services to include educational programs, cultural outings, and sports activities. These initiatives are designed not only to foster skills and confidence but also to steer our youth away from social disorder and crime, ensuring they contribute positively to the community.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower the youth of South Shields by providing educational, cultural, and recreational activities that promote personal growth, community cohesion, and steer them clear of antisocial behaviours. Vision: To be a transformative force in the lives of young people, enabling them to become active, informed, and culturally aware citizens who contribute positively to our diverse society.

Team

BYO is driven by a committed team of community leaders, seasoned professionals, and enthusiastic volunteers dedicated to the welfare and advancement of our young members. Our leadership team comprises individuals from various sectors including education, social work, and community service, ensuring a comprehensive and inclusive approach to our programs and initiatives. Our volunteers, the backbone of our organisation, offer mentorship, tutoring, and coaching, playing a crucial role in the success of each event and activity.
